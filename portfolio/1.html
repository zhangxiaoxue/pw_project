A Question & Answer Platform about Agriculture Technology

This is a platform designed for farmers and agronomists, where questions about crop planting, pest control, fertilization techniques and so forth can be asked. Afterwards these question will be answered by agriculture professors. This project is the few websites in China aiming at helping the traditional agriculture industry.

I was responsible for the front-end development.
